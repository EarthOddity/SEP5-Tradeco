Circular Economy

A new model that has arrived, not to break the mould, but to renovate it.

Circular Economy is a pattern of production and consumption where the materials and products in the economic cycle are kept with the highest possible value for as long as possible. It focuses on the creation of circular habits, such as repurposing or reusing. Also, it is about demolishing the barriers between individuals to create a cooperative chain that will ensure a greater future.
